Deandre Swift is the RB3 on the season

We're at the point in the season where the statistical rank of a player starts to have more weight.

I was incredibly impressed by Swift in the Rams game. As you can see from this picture, the competition was stiff. But Swift would make the defender miss time and time again and rip off big plays, almost willing his inferior team to victory.

I will never again doubt a bad situation in fantasy. When you have juice, you make plays. Swift has it. And the lions haven't had a back like this since Barry Sanders. He looks like he isn't going anywhere as a top flight RB.
