James Conner just dropped 40

During the draft, I remember Devin turning to me in like the eight round, "Should I grab Jonnu Smith here?"

"No", I said seriously. "You should grab a running back".

We look at the top available running backs..

"James Conner?" Devin asked me

"Nah, I'd probably go with a different running back (offer something obscure)"

"I'm going with Conner" - Dkazz

Devin had the wisdom to seek my counsel, but also the discernment to reject the lousy part of it

He landed James Conner and now that Edmonds is hurt, and Kyler is banged up, and the Cardinals are somehow still the best team, it goes through Conner.

Conner was already a star in his role getting goal line carries. He did enough to earn a start in Devin's lineup before exploding.

I thought he looked good even before he had truly shown it, but I rejected him in trade offers, as I'm sure many of you did.

But at least I got to keep the gift that keeps on giving with OBJ.
