Davante Adampants.

Why do we call him that?

There's this guy with some audiotapes on youtube with some of my favorite videos. He goes by the name Jonathan Adampants. I always recommend him so last year when it was my turn to draft Sven suggested I take Adams and name my team Davonte Adampants. I went with Chubb instead. It's a missed opportunity that will always haunt me.

Drafting Adams is tricky because you end up without a stud running back in most situations. However, it takes a truly special player to be dubbed the number one guy, have defenses circle you throughout the week, and still deliver value. It's remarkable to me that Adams continues to beat the double team. Some of it is definitely Rodgers, but when Rodgers misses games Adams continues to dominate.

He's a red zone threat, he's a possession receiver, and he's a deep threat. There's nothing he can't do. He's fast, physical and is also the best route runner in the league. His gold Air Jordan gloves really suit him, as he has golden hands and the mad hops right now to back it up.
