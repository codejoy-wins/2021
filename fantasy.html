Lamar Jackson finally beat the chiefs.

During the pregame show they went around and every single analyst picked the chiefs.

That's why we watch, as my dad always says. We don't know what will happen.

Even with fourth string running backs and practice squad players, the combination of Lamar Jackson in the backfield is just too much for defenses to defend. The Ravens ended up getting massive running lanes at will.

You look at the other side of the ball and even with the threat of Patrick Mahomes arm, the running game can't get going. Maybe that's because the Ravens are so good up front on both sides of the ball, but I really think it's the Talent of Lamar Jackson and the willingness to run. Lamar won't do this every game. He wouldn't last the season healthy. But against the chiefs, to get the monkey off his back, he was unstoppable.

Of Course, Mahomes still had victory ready to go until Clyde Edwards Nowhere coughed it up, but credit to Lamar Jackson.

If He's trying his hardest to run and use his full range of athleticism I don't think there's a more dangerous QB than Lamar. However, I also think it's not sustainable. And the perfect quarterback is like Russell Wilson who's both elusive and devestating with his arm. That's what makes Mahomes so good -- He has sneaky athleticism but saves it for big moments. That's also why I like Kyler, but nobody is a better runner than Lamar at QB in the history of football and that's the hardest thing to defend hands down. It's just double edged because if he gets hurt they're screwed.
