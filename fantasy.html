Patrick Mahomes is back to his ways.

Gentlemen, this will be this season's final power rankings because there are only 2 teams remaining, clashing in a championship of epic proportions. Not only that, but these are the 2 powerhouses of our league, and this is a proper championship.

The chiefs are superbowl favorties and Pat Mahomes keeps putting up nice points in fantasy football. Ernie had Melvin Gordon and Darrel Henderson combine for 2 points and Ernie put up 160+ anyway. How? Because he benched them. He somehow picked up Ronald Jones and with Fournette injured that's a slam dunk 16 points. Then he also has the luxury of starting Nick Chubb.

There's a lot of fantasy teams out there that lost because Kelce got Covid, but not Ernie. He's got a TD from Dawson Knox whenever he wants.

Andrew, however, has the TE1 on the season in Mark Andrews, putting up Kelce numbers and then some.

Andrew won his playoff game on the back of Mark Andrews and on the arm of Pat Mahomes. Which is crazy when He has 3 top 10 RBs in Mixon, Patterson, and Harris. Mixon went off and will continue to do so as long as he's healthy and so is Joe Burrow. The Bengals might actually win the super bowl this year, but I'd put my money on the Bucs, Chiefs, or Packers.

Andrew has St Brown going off on the lions and Patterson struggling, so that will be an interesting decision. I would honestly go St Brown at this point in a PPR flex. He's not a rookie anymore, it's the end of the season.

I honestly don't know who's going to win. I have $50 on Ernie but Andrew has proven his tactical genius by playing with the fantasy gods and throwing his game against me. If he hadn't done that, he would have been eliminated by Ernie last week.

That's all, guys. Thank you for saying thank you. It's my pleasure.
