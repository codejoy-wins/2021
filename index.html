About me

I am a white blood cell.

I used to think I had something to share with the world that they needed to know but now I realize I'm just a person having my own experience and expressing myself.

The beauty of my philosophy is that if you do not see the problems I see, don't worry. I view you as an equally valuable red blood cell.

My philosophical aim is to inspire all people to see themselves as cells of a living God, and to act accordingly.

A traffic jam to this God would be like a blood clot to us.

I see an infection, and you don't have to see it if you're a red blood cell. We're on the same team.

My goal is to one day cure the illness I see in our society and make a positive contribution to a better one. It's possible capitalism is not the final solution. I think sinister forces exist and are in control and manipulating the masses with mind control, but right now I'm just trying to make my own way and carve out a nice little life before I try to solve all the world's problems.

The truth is you have to sustain both yourself and the system. Because you are part of the system. Do not let yourself battle between helping yourself and helping others. Do both.

By making a positive impact on yourself you make the most positive impact on the system because you carry more impact weight when you're working on yourself than you do when you're working on other people.

Glory to God, And I love all my haters.
