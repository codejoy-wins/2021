About me

I am hard to figure out.

This website started out as a project called 2021 where I had kind of stopped coding for a while so I came up with a plan to make as good of a myname.com as I could.

I could compile all of my work onto it and kind of organize it and make it available.

It's a metaproject in a sense, and now that it's 2022, I'm still editing a folder called 2021.

maxjann.com/blog redirects to maxjann.com/the current month. So I already have a March.html for example with my last years blog posts from march. And then I can blog on top of that.

The blog is starting to have astrological vibes because when it's march 2022 I get a chance to reflect on March 2021. I wrote some crazy shit last year haha. But I just think it's cool that the blog writes itself. I only made one blog post this month in March but maxjann.com/blog has plenty of fresh material because it redirects to the current month and gives you all that content.

I'm constantly experimenting with different ways to use this website but at the beginning of last year I built a skeleton that I know how to modify. I knew I would be able to understannd how it works from the inside out and be able to build out the nervous system as I see fit.

Now I'm interested in giving it AI which is why I make it seem like the website knows what time it is and is socially aware of the weekend.

The thoughts that I express on here are experimental and may be alarming or offensive to you and I would recommend you read 1984 and take a psychadelic at least once in your life if possible before reading. Take what you like and leave the rest and think your own thoughts.

I like using my website and I hope you do too.
