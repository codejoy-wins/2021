"It's like passing on Michael Jordan."

Said Dabo Sweeney, head football coach of the Clemson Tigers when asked what he thought about teams potentially passing on Deshaun Watson in the draft.

Deshaun is one of those "too good to be true" prospects that gets knocked down draft boards for no reason. There's really no flaw to his game. In my opinion, he is the perfect modern quarterback. He led the league in passing last year after losing DeAndre Hopkins and having the worst roster in the league.

Patrick Mahomes is defintely special, but Deshaun Watson makes everyone look good. How does Patrick Mahomes make guys that aren't Tyreek and Kelce look? Meanwhile Deshuan Watson will extend the play and make Akins look like a good tight end and Will Fuller look like Randy Moss. He is next level.

It's extremely rare to have a quarterback who can both run as well as watson but also throw as well as him. The only other qb that comes to mind is Russell Wilson. Maybe Kyler, this year. But I think Watson reigns supreme. I think he's the number one most talented QB in the leauge.

I was just looking at Ernie's team to do this power rankings and noticed Deshaun on his squad. I read the news, potential dolphins trade this week. The fact that I just traded Jaylen Waddle for Njokes on me is painful already, but having lost Watson too? Icing on the cake of pain. I drafted Watson in the 10th round. I googled him every day for like a month. I was following the story but I couldn't keep the faith.

The worst drop I ever had was Julius Thomas at TE right before kickoff of week 1. I could see it, but the experts couldn't, and I let their projections influence me. Then Peyton Manning comes out as a Bronco and throws 3 touchdowns to him and came out and said, "If you can't get the ball to Julius Thomas as a quarterback then something is wrong with you." Manning set the passing TD record that season, and Julius had maybe 20 of them. Undrafted TE in fantasy. He was on my team, and I dropped him and struggled at tight end all year.

Fantasy is a maddening experience. Winning is so fun. I hope everyone in this league wins at least one time to know what it feels like. Losing is the rule. Winning is the exception. This does not seem to be my year, but neverless, I hope it is the year of Deshaun Watson. Personally, I'm a conspiracy theorist and don't believe jack shit that's reported. I think Deshaun is innocent. I don't think he's a bad person. He always struck me as a good person and a Christian. He donated his paycheck to the cafeteria workers and was always extremely nice. I don't know him personally of course. Just my take on it.
