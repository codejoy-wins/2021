Climbing with rhyming is all about timing

Dancing with poems is all about tone

Desire like fire and love like a glove

The Moon is a mirror and the sun wants a hug


Alluring imploring is perfectly boring

So here is the answer to keep you from snoring

Think of your wish and write it on paper

Congruent life actions will make it your later
